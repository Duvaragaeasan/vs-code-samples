Weeping

by Federico GarAa Locra

Weeping,
I go down the street
Grotesque, without solution
With the sadness of Cyrano
And Quixote.



Redeeming
Infinite impossiblities not working.
With the rhythm of the clock.

String Bass

by Adrian Green

Some like to dominate,
others caress
a voluptuous rhythm
on pliant strings.
This pulse drives life
through wanton counterpoint.
the hear and harmony
of strings.
